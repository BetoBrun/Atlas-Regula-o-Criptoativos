# Philippines

## Overview
The Philippines has steadily formalized VASP-style oversight and remains an important case of emerging-market licensing.

## Regulatory model
Emerging-market licensing model with increasing formalization.

## Stablecoins
Stablecoins remain more operational than deeply prudential.

## Tokenization
Tokenization is active but still secondary.

## CBDC
CBDC is under debate and pilot-oriented.
