# Thailand

## Overview
Thailand combines exchange licensing, securities-style controls, and active supervisory engagement with digital-asset markets.

## Regulatory model
Licensed market model with active SEC-style oversight.

## Stablecoins
Stablecoin treatment is still evolving and tied to payment-system concerns.

## Tokenization
Tokenization is active, especially in controlled pilot settings.

## CBDC
CBDC work emphasizes wholesale and cross-border experimentation.
