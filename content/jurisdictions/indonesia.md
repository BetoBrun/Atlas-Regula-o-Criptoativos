# Indonesia

## Overview
Indonesia has moved gradually, balancing crypto market participation with prudential caution and capital-flow sensitivities.

## Regulatory model
Gradual formalization under multiple authorities.

## Stablecoins
Stablecoin treatment remains partial and linked to broader payment concerns.

## Tokenization
Tokenization is developing, but not yet a central competitive edge.

## CBDC
CBDC remains exploratory within broader digital rupiah work.
