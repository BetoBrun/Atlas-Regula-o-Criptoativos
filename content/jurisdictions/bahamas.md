# Bahamas

## Overview
Open offshore-friendly licensing for digital asset businesses, with focus on registration, custody, and compliance.

## Regulatory model
Offshore licensed framework with supervisory gating and compliance controls.

## Stablecoins
Rules-based entry for stablecoin and custodial businesses, but not a global benchmark for payment-scale issuance.

## Tokenization
Tokenization is allowed through broader digital asset and securities-compatible structuring.

## CBDC
The Bahamas also offers a live CBDC experience through the Sand Dollar, relevant for retail CBDC comparison.
