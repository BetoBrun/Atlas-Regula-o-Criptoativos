# Cayman Islands

## Overview
Cayman provides a structured but internationally oriented registration and supervision environment for virtual asset services.

## Regulatory model
Internationally oriented licensed regime focused on service providers and compliance.

## Stablecoins
Stablecoin-related businesses fit the service-provider logic, though systemic payment integration is limited.

## Tokenization
Tokenization is generally feasible through fund, SPV, and digital-asset structures.

## CBDC
CBDC is not central to the jurisdictional strategy.
