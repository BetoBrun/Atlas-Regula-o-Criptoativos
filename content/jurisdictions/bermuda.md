# Bermuda

## Overview
Bermuda combines dedicated digital asset licensing with an insurance and funds-friendly regulatory environment.

## Regulatory model
Specialized digital asset framework tied to a credible supervisory authority.

## Stablecoins
Stablecoin issuance is generally possible within the licensed perimeter and supervisory expectations.

## Tokenization
Tokenization is treated as an extension of digital asset business and securities structuring.

## CBDC
No major retail CBDC agenda drives the framework.
