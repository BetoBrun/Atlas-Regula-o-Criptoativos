# Taiwan

## Overview
Taiwan has been building a more structured digital-asset perimeter while keeping close watch on consumer protection and market conduct.

## Regulatory model
Structured, still-maturing framework with conduct emphasis.

## Stablecoins
Stablecoin rules remain less mature than in Singapore or Japan.

## Tokenization
Tokenization is active and institutionally plausible.

## CBDC
CBDC remains exploratory.
