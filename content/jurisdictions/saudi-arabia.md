# Saudi Arabia

## Overview
Saudi Arabia is engaging more actively with digital assets through controlled policy development and payment-system experimentation.

## Regulatory model
Controlled reform path with emphasis on institutional use cases.

## Stablecoins
Stablecoins are under development rather than fully operationalized.

## Tokenization
Tokenization is active in pilot and institutional settings.

## CBDC
CBDC work emphasizes wholesale and cross-border cooperation.
