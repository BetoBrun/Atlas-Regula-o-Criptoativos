# Mauritius

## Overview
Mauritius has pursued an internationally oriented digital-asset posture with emphasis on licensing and financial-center competitiveness.

## Regulatory model
Competitiveness-driven licensing and service-provider model.

## Stablecoins
Stablecoin regulation is still less mature than in leading jurisdictions.

## Tokenization
Tokenization is possible but still secondary to service licensing.

## CBDC
CBDC remains secondary.
