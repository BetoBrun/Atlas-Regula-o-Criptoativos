# Ukraine

Ukraine remains a high-adoption case where war, payments, and digital finance have accelerated regulatory and operational relevance.

## Regulatory model
High-adoption reforming framework under exceptional conditions.

## Stablecoins
Stablecoins are operationally important, even though the legal framework is still maturing.

## Tokenization
Tokenization is developing but secondary to immediate financial utility.
