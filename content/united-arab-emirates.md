# Turkey

Turkey reflects high usage pressure, inflation-driven demand, and an evolving but still incomplete regulatory perimeter.

## Regulatory model
Adoption-rich but still frictional framework.

## Stablecoins
Stablecoins are central to use cases, but regulation remains incomplete.

## Tokenization
Tokenization is still incipient.

## CBDC
CBDC remains in pilot and policy discussion.
