# Liechtenstein

## Overview
Liechtenstein offers one of the clearest token economy statutes in Europe, centered on legal wrappers and service-provider certainty.

## Regulatory model
Comprehensive token economy law with clear service-provider categories.

## Stablecoins
Stablecoins fit within a legally structured token system, though scale remains limited.

## Tokenization
Tokenization is one of the jurisdiction’s strongest comparative advantages.

## CBDC
CBDC is not a central pillar of the model.
